<p>Singapore has established itself as Asia's leading biomedical sciences
hub, hosting world-class research facilities and manufacturing operations
of major pharmaceutical companies. The sector continues to grow with increasing
focus on cell and gene therapy, medical technology, and digital health
solutions. With substantial government investment in research and development,
and the presence of leading companies like GSK, Novartis, and Sanofi, this
sector offers rewarding careers in research, manufacturing, and regulatory
affairs.</p>
<p>Curious about making your mark in biomedical sciences? Connect with our
VCAs who are shaping Singapore's thriving biopharmaceutical landscape.</p>
