<p>Singapore's hospitality and tourism sector is reinventing itself with
new attractions, integrated resorts, and unique experiences. The industry
emphasises high-value tourism and sustainable practices, creating opportunities
in hotel management, MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions),
tourism technology, and experience design. With Singapore's position as
a premier destination for leisure and business travellers, the sector offers
exciting careers in guest services, event management, and tourism development.</p>
<p>Want to be part of Singapore's vibrant hospitality scene? Let our VCAs
share their experiences and guide you towards exciting opportunities in
this dynamic industry.</p>
