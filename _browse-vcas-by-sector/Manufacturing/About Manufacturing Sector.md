<p>Singapore's manufacturing sector is evolving with Industry 4.0, incorporating
advanced robotics, IoT, and smart manufacturing solutions. The sector spans
electronics, precision engineering, chemicals, and biomedical manufacturing.
With increasing focus on high-value manufacturing and sustainability, professionals
can find opportunities in advanced manufacturing, supply chain innovation,
and green manufacturing practices.</p>
