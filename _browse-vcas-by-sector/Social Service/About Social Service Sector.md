<p>Singapore's social service sector is growing to meet evolving community
needs. The sector focuses on inclusive support systems and innovative social
solutions. From family services to elderly care, professionals can make
meaningful impact through roles in social work, programme development,
community engagement, and social innovation. The sector offers opportunities
to contribute to Singapore's social fabric while developing professional
expertise.</p>
